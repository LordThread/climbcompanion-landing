What you can do with Climb Companion

Projects

Follow a route or a boulder across multiple sessions: attempts, notes, photos and progress in one place.

Manual training

Create your own exercises and training cards, log sets, reps and loads exactly the way you want.

Sessions

Category breakdown, grade scale, volume and frequency — to really understand how you're progressing.

Challenges

Set personal goals and keep track of them over time, with reminders handled entirely by your phone.

No account. No server. No tracking.

Climb Companion has no server of its own: all your data stays exclusively on your device. No third-party SDKs, no advertising, no analytics watching you. The only way your data ever leaves your phone is a manual export you choose to make.
